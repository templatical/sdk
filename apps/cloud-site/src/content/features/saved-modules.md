## What it does

Your design system, ready to drop into any template.

- **Save any section** — Hero, footer, product grid, unsubscribe block. Promote to a reusable module in one click.
- **Drop anywhere** — Modules show up in the block sidebar alongside built-ins.
- **Workspace-wide** — Everyone on your team uses the same source. Update a module and the latest version is picked up the next time a template opens.

## Good fits

- Agencies shipping dozens of templates with a consistent footer
- Product teams with a branded hero used in every transactional email
- Anyone tired of rebuilding the same section from scratch
