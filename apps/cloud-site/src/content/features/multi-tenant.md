## What it does

Ship the editor inside your own SaaS with real customer isolation baked in.

- **Per-tenant API keys** — Every workspace authenticates with its own scoped key.
- **Data isolation** — Templates, media, snapshots, and comments never cross tenant boundaries.
- **Project scoping** — Group templates by product, region, or brand within a tenant.
- **JWT-based SDK auth** — Your backend mints short-lived tokens for end users. Cloud handles the rest.

## Good fits

- SaaS products embedding the editor for their own customers
- Agencies running a dozen clients from a single pane
- Enterprises with strict regional or BU-level data separation
