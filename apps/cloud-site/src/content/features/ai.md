## What it does

The AI Assistant lives inside the editor and covers three jobs your team actually does:

- **Generate from prompt** — Describe the email in a sentence. Get a structured template, not a wall of text. Blocks, sections, merge tags — already wired up.
- **Rewrite in place** — Select a paragraph, tweak tone, shorten, expand, or translate. Streaming output so you see the result as it’s written.
- **Design-to-template** — Drop a screenshot or a Figma frame. Get a matching template, styled to your brand tokens.

## Under the hood

Built on streaming LLM APIs with guardrails tuned for email. Generates content that respects your brand colors, font tokens, and available merge tags — not generic marketing copy.

## Good fits

- SaaS lifecycle emails: onboarding, renewal, retention
- Marketing ops producing weekly campaigns on deadline
- Agencies drafting client emails from a brief
