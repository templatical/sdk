## What it does

Version history for every template. No more "who changed the subject line?"

- **Automatic snapshots** — Every save creates a restorable version. No manual commits.
- **Named snapshots** — Tag a milestone ("pre-Black-Friday", "approved by legal") so you can find it later.
- **Side-by-side diff** — Compare any two versions visually. Restore a block or the whole thing.

## Good fits

- High-stakes sends where experimentation has to be reversible
- Compliance workflows needing an audit trail
- Teams with rotating reviewers who disagree in waves
