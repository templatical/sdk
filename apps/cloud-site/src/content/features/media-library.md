## What it does

A proper asset store for your emails — built by the same team, styled the same way.

- **Folders and search** — Organize images by campaign, brand, or team. Filter by type, date, or filename.
- **Drag-and-drop upload** — Paste from clipboard, drag from desktop, drop in bulk.
- **Crop, edit, replace** — Non-destructive edits, versioned replacement across every template using the asset.
- **CDN-backed** — Served from the edge. Fast load in the editor, fast render in the inbox.

## Good fits

- Teams reusing the same hero images across campaigns
- Brands enforcing a single source of truth for visuals
- Anyone tired of hunting for last quarter’s logo
