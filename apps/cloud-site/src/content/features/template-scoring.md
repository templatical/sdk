## What it does

Automated quality checks that catch mistakes before they reach the inbox.

- **Deliverability** — Spam-trigger phrases, image-to-text ratio, broken links, missing alt text.
- **Accessibility** — Color contrast (WCAG AA), heading structure, link clarity, font size minimums.
- **Brand consistency** — Flags off-brand colors, missing logos, tone drift against your style guide.

Every finding is actionable: score, severity, one-click fix where possible.

## Good fits

- Brand teams enforcing a style guide across a dozen campaigns
- Compliance-sensitive industries (finance, healthcare, legal)
- Anyone shipping to large lists where deliverability matters
