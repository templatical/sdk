## What it does

Expose your templates as a tool that Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible agent can drive.

- **Standard MCP server** — Works with any client in the [Model Context Protocol](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) ecosystem.
- **Safe, scoped operations** — Create, read, update, publish templates. No raw database access.
- **Audit trail** — Every agent action is logged with the originating agent, operation, and result.

## Why it matters

Your team doesn’t just use email tools — they use AI to use email tools. MCP turns your Templatical workspace into a first-class surface for agentic workflows.

## Good fits

- Engineering orgs standardizing on MCP for internal tooling
- Teams experimenting with AI-driven campaign automation
- Building custom agents that need programmatic template access
